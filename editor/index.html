About me.

I'm a film and television editor with over fifteen years' experience in a multitude of genres, including short and feature-length documentaries, scripted drama, docudramas, factual, and competition reality. I'm passionate about the craft of editing, and excel at finding innovative ways to create powerful, emotional stories. I consider it a privelege to collaborate with directors and producers to bring their visions to the screen. If you'd like to know more or would like to discuss working together, please get in touch.
